Return & Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping at Shopverse. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help.

1. Returns

You have 7 days to return an item from the date you received it. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in the original packaging.

2. Refunds

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status. If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. Refunds typically take 5–7 business days.

3. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it, please contact our support team with the order details.

4. Shipping

You will be responsible for paying your own shipping costs for returning the item. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions on how to return your item, please contact us at support@shopverse.com.
